  • Who...

    ...am I? Denver born, England educated, Nashville citizen. I first studied history and fine arts with a focus in cermics at Metropolitan State University of Denver, graduating Summa Cum Laude. Then I left for the UK and obtained my MA in archaeological and museum object conservation. It was this degree that brought me to Nashville, where I worked to help open the new Tennessee State Museum. I am now preparing for my future as full-stack MERN web developer through Vanderbilt University.
