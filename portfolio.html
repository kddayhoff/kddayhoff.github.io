Conservation Projects

Large Object Conservation

Conservation of large objects often requires a different approach. The big picture needs to be taken into account. The most important thing is to make it stable and prevent the need for frequent conservation.

Preventative Conservation

Preventing objects, large and small, from deteriorating is one of the most important aspect of conservation. Humidty, light, touching, etc. are just a few examples.

Restoration

Restoration is only part of conservation. It is returning the object to look and/or function as if it were brand new. This includes in-painting, adding new components to make it whole, and removing past conservation methods
